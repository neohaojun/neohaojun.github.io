CATmistry 👨‍🔬

iOS & iPadOS 📱

Chemistry, Gamified.

CATmistry was built to aid upper primary to lower secondary school students in their Chemistry learning journey. CATmistry aims to convey relatively simple Chemistry concepts in a simple and concise manner, and alongside the learn and information sections are mini-games and interactive elements to provide for a fun and engaging learning experience.

What I did

  • Mockup (Figma)
  • Development (Swift 5, Xcode 12)
  • Backend (Firebase)
  • App Store Screenshots (Figma)
