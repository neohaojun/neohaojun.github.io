In collaboration with HCI MakerSpace

Breakfast@MakerSpace is an initiative set up by the HCI MakerSpace team to give students opportunities to learn more from industry experts and even their peers on making, coding, tinkering, and STEM. In our various breakfast chat sessions throughout the year, students will be able to engage with our invited speakers and learn more about their journey in the relevant industries.

This session involved alumni of the Swift Accelerator Programme sharing about their journey, talk about the apps they created, and the skills they gained through their experience.
